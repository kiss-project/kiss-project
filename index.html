OPULUS

A Collaborative, Social Application To Help Align Working Teams And The Business On What Needs To Be Done While Cascading Budgets Seamlessly

OPULUS

A combination of the latin words opus and circulus - OPULUS represents the repeating cycle of work that budget owners and operational teams alike battle with on a quarterly basis. It evolves the process to expand from forecast periods alone but to live throughout the life of a project. By combining basic planning, budget requests and collaboration in one platform, OPULUS makes it easy to approve, align priorities and see budgets in real time.

The aim is to create a collaborative statement of work (SoW) and communicate directly between the budget owner and requesters. Its designed to break away from the traditional financial forecast process and to become a living collaborative platform directly between budget owners and working teams.

How It Works

OPULUS helps align everyone on a shared SoW and cascades the budgets in four simple steps

Budget owner defines their objectives and priorities
Teams request budget against authorised projects and iterate regularly in realtime
Budgets are consolidated - approved, rejected and discussed directly in OPULUS
Budgets are monitored and analysed by the owners than extracted as an aligned and consolidated view for finance
